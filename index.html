Stand up paddleboarding (SUP)

The term “SUP” is an acronym that comes from the English language – Stand Up Paddleboarding – paddling in a standing position and it is one of the fastest growing activities on the water. We are the first tourist agency in Croatia to start this activity in an organized way on the river. That is why our boards are specialized for navigating rivers, giving you the necessary stability to enjoy the activity without interruption.

The absolute advantage over all other activities is the flexibility, agility and speed of the SUP. It is perfect for exploring hidden backwaters, untouched lakes and flora and fauna that are not available to us in rafting boats.

Like any activity, it requires a certain amount of energy, but we believe that this is the goal of an active vacation. We found inspiration for our motto in the slogan of the American SEAL special forces, “no man left behind”, in translation, we will not allow you to sail to Hungary, Serbia, or for those who are more physically active, upstream to Slovenia. And not only because we care about our equipment, but also because the safety of you, the adventurer, is our absolute priority. Breaks are included in the activity, and at any time we can exchange the standing active position for lying passive on one of the pebble banks and catch a tan.

Rafting

Dive in the natural oasis of the river landscape of the “European Amazon”. Experience the river tributaries, bends and reefs and the habitats of the bald eagle, black stork, beaver and other inhabitants of the UNESCO protected Drava bioreserve from the enchanting perspective of the “Rafing Safari” downhill! There are 10 kilometers of crystal clear water, a beach full of pebbles perfect for sunbathing and swimming, and our skippers will make sure that the trip is aslo instructive, with whom you will learn the basics of rowing, get information about the equipment used, river flow and occurrences on rivers and on a safe way to stay on rivers.

Experience an unforgettable experience of adrenaline-fueled driving along the old Drava River in off-road vehicles for which there are no insurmountable obstacles. Kayak

40,00 EUR per person

Through the unique experience of kayaking, you will get to know the hidden parts of the Drava River with all the wealth of flora and fauna it hides. Driving through narrow channels, visiting hidden lakes, rapids and beautiful pebble beaches are the scenarios you will go through as part of your kayak adventure. Our guides have many years of experience in leading the group and are excellent connoisseurs of all the secrets that the Drava River hides, and all excursions have an educational character with the aim of introducing our guests to the protected flora and fauna of this unique ecosystem and strengthening awareness of the need to protect the environment. This active vacation will rest your soul, and you will carry memories with you for the rest of your life.

More adventure agency

The More Adventure travel agency was founded in 2017 with the aim of promoting the adventurous tourism of continental Croatia, which is often unfairly neglected. The seat of our agency, the city of Varaždin, is located on many traffic routes that every year a huge number of tourists from other parts of Europe lead south and we recognized the opportunity that the natural beauty and cultural sights of our region do not remain just another in a series of passing points . Our philosophy is simple – to convey our love of nature to you! From the first contact with you, we take care of logistics, equipment and security so that you can completely indulge in a vacation with your loved ones. Individual approach, high professionalism, expertise and flexibility of our agency will provide you with an unforgettable experience you will remember.
